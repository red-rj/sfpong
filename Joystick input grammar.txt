Joystick input grammar

joy_input   ::= "Joy" input_id
input       ::= button | axis
button      ::= "B" integer
axis        ::= axis_name axis_dir
axis_name   ::= "X" | "Y" | "Z" | "R" | "U" | "V" | "PovX" | "PovY"
axis_dir    ::= "+" | "-"
integer     ::= digit+
digit       ::= "0"..."9"


Exemples:
---
JoyB1, JoyB2, JoyB3, ... , JoyB32
JoyX+, JoyX-, JoyY+, JoyY-
JoyPovX+, JoyPovY-, JoyPovX-, JoyPovY+
